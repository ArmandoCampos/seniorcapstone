I Believe in False Motivation.
...
[ My "I Believe" Artifact ]
I believe in false motivation, and that motivation must be formed willingly and not spawned from an event unrelated to inner passion. To represent this belief I created a video to demonstrate the effect of false motivation through an animation. The video is purely an explanation of what I mean when I say false motivation, it is a visual demonstration of what happens when people fall for traps laid out in life. The process of creating the video was time consuming to say the least, hours were spent on just editing. Recording audio for the video was troublesome, but the visuals were enjoyable to create and not as frustrating. I would say the video was a proper representation of the belief and fully supported the idea I was going for.
