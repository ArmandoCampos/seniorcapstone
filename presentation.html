Creativity with Technology

~ Armando Campos ~
...
...
...
...
How come there is a lack of students who actively combine their creativity with computers, and what can be done to inspire and teach Poly students the ability to use computers as a creative tool?
...

...

First of all, why would you want to?
The benefits of having technical skills involving computers is important. There is high demand for those people who actually have the knowledge of how to create something.

For example, Websites. People want clean websites to represent their company, they are the demand, but the supply is limited because we may have millions of companies requiring a nice website but it isn't like we have millions of web developers. Any role in the Computer Science field is like this, we still aren't to that point yet where knowing a programming language is as common as having a fast food job, it's more like the rarity of being in the medical field. You can make a TON of money because of this.
